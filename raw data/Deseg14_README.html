ABOUT THIS DATA

This is a dataset of school desegregation orders. The invol_data file includes information about school desegregation orders mandated by federal courts. The vol_data file includes information about open school desegregation orders that resulted from voluntary agreements between school districts and the U.S. Department of Education’s Office of Civil Rights.

Voluntary orders are based on data received from the U.S. Department of Education and may not include open agreements entered into prior to 1996.

COLUMN DESCRIPTIONS

FOR vol_data file

State: The state that this agreement belongs to.

District Name: The name of the school district. Some school districts show up more than once because they have multiple orders.

Docket#: Unique identification number of the agreement.

Received: The date when the Department of Education received a complaint about this school district or opened an investigation on this school district.

Resolved: The date when the school district reached a desegregation agreement with the Department of Education.

FOR invol_openonly file

District Name: The name of the school district. Asterisk denoted a school district that is defunct as of the 2011-2012 school year due to merger or closure.

State: The state that this school desegregation court order belongs to.

City: The city that this school desegregation court order belongs to.

Year Lifted: The year when the desegregation court order was dismissed.

Year Placed: The year when the school district was placed under a desegregation order by a federal court.
